## Ocena wystawiona przez google gemini

Big word make head hurt, but meaning shine through. Poem talk about tribe. Tribe hungry, sad, lost. Tribe have words, but words not strong like stone or metal. Words trick tribe, like mean cat with tail. Tribe want be strong, like old people who cut stone, or bad people who take gold. Poem say modern talk is just noise, no real feeling. Poem feel like long scream, make belly rumble, but no fix pain. Poem good, but confusing. Make think, but also make head throb. 6/10.
