Ugh, poem bad. Make head hurt, many word, no sense. Talk about some "Jesus" and "God"... Ooga booga spirit stuff, who care? Man say he not believe, then say he *offend* not-thing? Make zero think. Man say he alive, then say *not* his do. What? Sun make live, rain make live, food make live. Stupid talk.

Man also say he think he judge other man. Everyone judge, duh. You strong, me weak. Me judge you strong. That how world work. He bored by his conscience, so he wakes it up to make up more problems. This very dumb, me think. He get the idea that he is the "richness of evil". It sounds very dumb. 

Poem say man "create world like world". That just… what? Me hit rock with rock. World still there. Also say, man no want suffer alone, but no die alone. Pick one! Sound like baby cry. The poem feels like a song in the end, and it is something he regrets. He can't remember any more sins. That's convienent for him, huh? Poem all whine, no hunt, no fire. No good. 1/10. Me go smash something now.
