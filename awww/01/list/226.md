## Ocena wystawiona przez google gemini

Big fire ball rise and fall, make hot and cold. Three man, Hank, other man, and Joe, walk far. Find yellow shiny, but no water. Joe weak, fall down. Hank and other man leave Joe. Other man weak, fall down. Wake up, see birds, see yellow shiny, but no water. Other man die, like Joe. Yellow shiny no help. Hank gone. Sun burn much. Poem make head hurt, but story clear... and very sad.

Poem is 3/10.
