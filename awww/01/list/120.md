## Ocena wystawiona przez google gemini

Big fire in sky, go round and round. Woman shake, clothes shiny. Yellow clothes scare, make soft bone hurt. Short hair, no long hair. Sneaky, quiet, listen good. Think fast, talk to tall man. Say "Me help you."

Night come, light go. Throw off scary clothes. Dust fight, make noise. Small man do work for big man. Big man kick, say "Work, or me hit!" Hurt go away, morning come. Put on old face, same as before. Grab shiny thing on door, same door day and day.

Poem make head hurt little. Words funny, hard think. See picture in head, though. Not best poem, not worst poem. 6/10.
