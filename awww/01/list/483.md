## Ocena wystawiona przez google gemini

Big fire sticks walk in smoky air. Fire sticks show we are we. Take fire sticks through dark, no leave hands. Fire sticks make us go, keep fire alive. Fire not from wood, not from ground goo, but red water, red water. 

No shiny rock or sharp stick in fire, only dark on face. Fire sticks must live long, watch burn down low. Maybe see sun, then see no fire stick, only hand burn. Shake off black dust, go all ways, no power. Burn in walk, no know who. Breathe heavy to fire, to dark, to smoke. Poem good, make think. Fire hurt, but show truth. 7/10.
