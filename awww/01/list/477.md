## Ocena wystawiona przez google gemini

Big water all around. Cold drink from far away, smell good air in morning. Woman in shiny dress say words from old place. She old, like many suns. She see water many times, wait for man from her home.

She say more words from old time, mean love strong, never die. Rocks sit, birds make noise. Woman sad, dress not shiny now. Water place make sad sound. 

Poem make feel... thinky. Make think about long time, far place, and sad heart. Not best poem, but not worst. Poem 6/10.
