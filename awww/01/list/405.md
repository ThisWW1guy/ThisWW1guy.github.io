## Ocena wystawiona przez google gemini

Poem talk about pretty she-thing and ugly he-thing. She-thing sleep, smooth like water. He-thing rough, like rock and dirt. He-thing maybe hurt she-thing, make sad red water. Poem ask why she-thing sleep so easy. Maybe she-thing know strong, even when sleep. He-thing maybe scared, but still do bad. She-thing like big earth-mother, make all things. He-thing come from she-thing, even if he-thing bad. Poem confusing, many strange words. Make think hard, but good think. 6/10.
